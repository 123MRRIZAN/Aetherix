Project & Team

Aetherix is an educational and interactive platform that combines climate science, mathematics, and artificial intelligence. It allows users to explore real-time atmospheric data, calculate their personal carbon footprint, and understand how AI can contribute to sustainable solutions. Designed for students, educators, and climate enthusiasts, Aetherix demonstrates the power of technology in achieving SDG 13: Climate Action, helping users make informed decisions for a greener planet.
