Mathematics Behind Aetherix

Mathematics is the foundation of Aetherix — every climate model, AI prediction, and sustainability analysis relies on mathematical principles. This page demonstrates how equations and data visualization support SDG 13: Climate Action through measurable insights.

1️⃣ Carbon Emission Formula

The carbon footprint is calculated using:
CO₂ = Distance (km) × Emission Factor (kg CO₂/km)

2️⃣ Visualizing Climate Trends

Mathematical graphs help represent how CO₂ emissions grow over time. The line below represents an increasing trend in emissions and can be modeled using a linear equation:
y = mx + c where m is the rate of increase and c is the base level.

3️⃣ Predictive Modelling with AI

Artificial Intelligence uses mathematical operations like matrix multiplication and gradient descent to predict future patterns. These are built on algebra, calculus, and statistics — all fundamental branches of mathematics that make intelligent predictions possible.

4️⃣ Optimization & Reduction

Using optimization algorithms, AI can help minimize energy waste and emissions. This directly relates to mathematical techniques like minimization of functions and derivative-based optimization.
